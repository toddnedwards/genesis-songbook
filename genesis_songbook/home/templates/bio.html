Meet The Genesis Songbook Members

Celebrating the rich musical legacy of one of the UK’s biggest selling bands. Featuring former members of Genesis Visible Touch - the vocal and musical talents of Chris O’Connell (And Finally Phil Collins) and Tony Turrell (Fish, Thomas Dolby, Francis Dunnery, It Bites FD), alongside other professional musicians with a passion for the music of Genesis. Let us take you through the Songbook of Phil Collins fronted Genesis!

Chris O'Connell

Chris is a lifelong Genesis fan from Liverpool. A multi-instrumentalist, Chris’ singing voice has an uncanny resemblance to that of Phil Collins. Chris also has a background in theater and acting, skills that he brings to the Genesis Songbook live show.

John Lovegrove

A talented player and singer, John started playing guitar at the tender age of eight, and in addition to Genesis claims that he also counts Pink Floyd and George Formby among his influences. This suggests that, whenever staring out upon six saintly shrouded men, he at least has the luxury of doing so through clean windows.

Leon Parr

Leon started drumming at school at the age of 13 and on leaving school went onto Leigh College and then on to Leeds College of Music between 93 – 96 where he studied for a degree in Jazz. He has been a pro since about 1996, with the usual mix of gigging and teaching. He spent most of the 90’s in a band called Mr So and So which supported Marillion on their 1997 U.K tour.
